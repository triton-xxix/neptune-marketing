## 13. LinkedIn Social Selling Index (SSI) Boosters

**The 4 Pillars of SSI:**

1. **Establish your professional brand**
   - Complete profile (photo, headline, about, experience)
   - Share relevant content weekly
   - Get endorsements and recommendations

2. **Find the right people**
   - Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator (if budget allows)
   - Target by: Industry, Company size, Job title, Location
   - Save leads and accounts

3. **Engage with insights**
   - Comment meaningfully on prospect posts
   - Share industry news with your take
   - Post original content consistently

4. **Build relationships**
   - Accept relevant connection requests
   - Send personalized connection requests
   - Follow up after connections
   - Move conversations to calls/meetings
